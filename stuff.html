ULAB Computer Society Club

Mission & Vision

ULAB ComSoc is a club of IT-savvy ULABians. It hopes to be a club, where students interested about technologies can share their knowledge and be benefited from each other by collaboration. ULAB ComSoc is also keen to provide a platform for developing leadership skills.
ULAB ComSoc arranges various workshops, training sessions, seminars based on digital technology. Those workshops and seminars focus new technologies and their impacts. These activities hopefully make its members smarter and enable them to remain updated and prepare them for future responsibilities. We organize programming contest, gaming contest, math Olympiad; participate in cultural programs like club day.

ULAB has IEEE ULAB Student Branch with a large numbers of students. IEEE is the world's largest professional organization dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

Alumni

Alumni associations are societies of graduates or, more broadly, of former students. Alumni associations organize social events, publish newsletters, and raise funds for the organization or their alma mater. Alumni associations offer a variety of benefits and services that help alumni maintain connections to their educational institution and fellow graduates. Additionally, such groups often support new alumni, and provide a forum to form new friendships and business relationships with people of similar background.

Objectives of the ULAB Alumni Association
  • Establish fellowship among ULAB graduates
  • Maintain links with ULAB after graduation
